# Phase 5 Wave 2 retrospective

## Outcome

Wave 2 completed as the discovery/template packet.

## What landed

1. single-source metadata remains tied to owner planes
2. task packet creation now has a reusable plan/spec/task/evidence/retro template
3. discovery and reconciliation are bounded by convergence rules rather than free-form sync

## What changed in the operating model

Creating new wave packets or future task-center discovery logic now follows an explicit template chain instead of ad hoc document and task generation.
